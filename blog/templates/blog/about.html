About Us

This is a safe and anonymous storytelling platform designed to help individuals share personal experiences and thoughts related to mental health. Inspired by classic social media simplicity, it provides a minimal yet powerful space for expression and support.

Users can sign up quickly with just a username and password—no email verification needed—ensuring ease of access while preserving privacy. This project also serves as a demonstration of modern DevOps practices using Docker and containerized deployment.

Features:

  • Anonymous Profiles with Custom Bios
  • Timeline of Real, Raw Stories from Users
  • Simple, Secure Story Posting Interface
